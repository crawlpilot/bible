## Principal-Level Framing

- Object-oriented design is about controlling complexity through boundaries and responsibilities.
- Good OO design improves changeability, testability, and readability.
- Bad OO design creates deep inheritance trees, hidden coupling, and classes that know too much.
- The goal is not to use every pattern. The goal is to make the right trade-off for the problem.
