# Stakeholder Communication

Principal engineers communicate upward to executives, sideways to peer leaders, and downward to teams — each with a different register, cadence, and goal. This section covers templates and frameworks for all three.

## Principles

- **Pyramid principle**: lead with the ask or conclusion, then support
- **Audience calibration**: executives want risk and business impact; peers want trade-offs; teams want clarity and context
- **One metric per update**: never bury the signal in data
- **Pre-wiring**: alignment conversations happen before the meeting, not during
- **Written > verbal**: async-first communication scales better than status meetings
