## Principal Engineer Roadmap Responsibilities

At the principal level, you own the **technical roadmap** for a domain or platform — not as a Gantt chart maker, but as the person who:

- Translates business strategy into a sequence of technical investments
- Surfaces the dependencies and risks leadership doesn't see
- Makes trade-offs explicit: "if we do X, we cannot do Y this quarter"
- Evolves the roadmap as context changes — without losing credibility
- Aligns 5–10 teams around a shared technical direction

**The roadmap is your primary influence artifact.** Engineers without authority shape direction through the quality of their plans.
